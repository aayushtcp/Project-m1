About HCC

Hetauda City College is a prestigious Information Technology college in Nepal. The college is in the heart of Hetauda, which is easily accessible from both the Terai and the hilly regions of Nepal. Currently, the college offers a variety of academic programs, including 10+2 in Science, Management, and Hotel Management faculties, as well as Bachelor programs in Information Technology.

The college began modestly, with thirty-three students in the Management stream at the high school level. Today, it has over 1000 students studying in over fourteen academic programs each year. The college now has highly experienced faculty, advanced infrastructure, and technological advancements, ensuring that students receive a world-class education.

In the Higher Secondary level, we offer courses in Science and Management. A Bachelor of Computer Application is part of our Undergraduate program. Hetauda City College, with the motto "Real Word Problem Solving," is committed to providing quality education for the advancement of society.

Mission

The purpose of HCC is to offer a platform for experience design thinking so that problems can be turned into opportunities.

Vision

HCC aspires to be Nepal's top institution of higher learning for immersive, innovative, and transformative education in the twenty-first century.

Philosophy

Transform the Challenges into Opportunities is the Hetauda City College's (HCC) guiding philosophy.

Values

Positive discipline, integrity, respect for variety, ongoing improvement, and sustainability are among HCC's core values.
